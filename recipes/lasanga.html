Lasanga

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food—a warm, cheesy, saucy, and satisfying dish that feels like a hug in every bite. This easy version layers lasagna sheets with a rich meat or veggie sauce, creamy white béchamel, and lots of gooey cheese. Whether you're cooking for a family dinner or treating yourself on a weekend, this baked classic is always a crowd-pleaser. Don’t worry—it sounds fancy but is totally doable at home!

Ingredients:

  • 6 lasagna sheets (pre-boiled or oven-ready)
  • 1 cup pasta sauce (tomato-based)
  • 200g ground meat or paneer (for vegetarian version)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella or processed cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1½ cups milk
  • Salt, pepper, oregano to taste

Instructions:

  1. White sauce: Melt butter, stir in flour, then slowly add milk while whisking. Cook till thick. Add salt and pepper.

  2. Meat/veggie sauce: Sauté onion, add meat/paneer, pour in pasta sauce, cook for 5-7 mins. Add oregano.

  3. In a greased dish, layer: lasagna sheet → meat sauce → white sauce → cheese. Repeat.

  4. Top with more cheese.

  5. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25-30 minutes, until golden and bubbly.

Done! Rest for 5 minutes, then slice and serve your hearty homemade lasagna 🍝🧀
