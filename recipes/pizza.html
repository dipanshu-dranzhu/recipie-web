La Pizza

This quick and easy homemade pizza is perfect for busy days or late-night cravings. Using a ready-made base like naan or pizza bread, you can whip up a cheesy, crispy, and delicious pizza in just minutes. It’s fully customizable with your favorite veggies or toppings and makes a fun cooking activity for both kids and adults. Whether it’s a snack or a light dinner, this pizza always hits the spot!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ready-made pizza base (or naan)
  • 3 tbsp pizza sauce (or tomato ketchup + herbs)
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ onion, sliced
  • ½ bell pepper, sliced
  • 4-5 olives (optional)
  • Pinch of oregano and chili flakes
  • 2 eggs
  • Meat slices

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).

  2. Meat/veggie sauce: Sauté onion, add meat/paneer, cook for 5-7 mins. Add oregano.

  3. Add onions, bell peppers, meat and eggs.

  4. Top with more cheese.

  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until cheese melts and edges turn golden.

Done! Slice, serve, and enjoy your melty, homemade pizza 🍕✨
